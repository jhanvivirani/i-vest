What if we streamline

i-Vest gives you money to pay for college. In return, we ask you to pay us a small percent of your salary after you graduate for a fixed period of time (usually 3% - 8% for three to five years). That's it.

How It Works

Students


We partner directly with colleges to integrate i-Vest into their finanical aid systems.

Once you apply through your school's finanical aid office and get approved, they authorize you to set up your i-Vest account.

We deposit the money into your school's term bill. After you graduate, you pay us from your i-Vest account monthly like you would pay a credit card bill.

Investors


Anyone can invest in a student's education.

Interested investors can set up an i-Vest Invest account and choose to invest in any of our partnering colleges.

Investors get their returns over the course of 6-8 years in the form of annual installments.

Features

Income-Adjusted Payments

How much you pay back monthly depends on how much income you make, so you can get an education knowing huge bills won't come back to haunt you.

Debt-Free Guarantee

After you finish your time making payments, you're student debt free. It doesn't even matter if you paid off how much you borrowed. You're done.

Flexibility

Life happens. We allow for you to defer your payments if you lose your job or go back to school, so you don't have to worry about your student loans during these times. We also make payments optional for those who make less than $30,000 per year.

People Funding People

Our competitors get their funds exclusively from investment firms and wealthy private investors. That's not really our speed. Our user-friendly platform lets anyone open an i-Vest Invest account and invest in i-Vest funds in participating universities.




A Win-Win For Everyone

Whether you want to help out students at your alma mater or just looking to diversify your porfolio, i-Vest Invest accounts are projected to provide a higher return on investment than most CDs and safe bonds.
